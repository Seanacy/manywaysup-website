Stabilization · BridgeWork

Fund Community Tasks with BridgeWork

BridgeWork connects people facing financial hardship with small, same-day tasks — yard work, moving help, cleaning, errands — that pay $15 to $20 for 15 to 20 minutes of work. No background check. No application. No waiting. Just a way to earn cash today.

This campaign funds the task pool that makes that possible.

How It Works

Your contribution becomes someone's next paycheck.

01

You contribute

Any amount you give goes into the BridgeWork task fund, held in escrow — not spent until a task is actually completed.

02

A task gets posted

Someone facing hardship picks up a same-day task — a 15 to 20 minute job that pays $15 to $20.

03

Money moves on completion

Once the task is verified done, the payout releases from escrow straight to the worker. That's it.

🔒

Every dollar is held in escrow through Stripe and only released when a task is verified complete. The campaign runs on WeStackr, our own crowdfunding platform, which tracks a public reputation score, completion rate, and payout history for full transparency — you can see exactly where the money goes.
