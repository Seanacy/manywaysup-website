What's Next

Building Toward What's Next

Many Ways Up doesn't wait until something is finished to talk about it. Here's what's in early development right now — ideas that aren't built yet, but are on the way.

In the Notebook

More Are Coming.

Concept

Neighbors Grow

A future platform connecting community gardeners with neighbors facing food insecurity.

Neighbors Grow will connect people with extra space in their garden to neighbors who need fresh food nearby. The idea started with a single tomato plant and a note left on a doorstep. The concept is written down. The build is coming.

Concept

Halo Housing Project

A verified rent assistance platform connecting donors directly to families facing eviction.

Halo Housing Project will give donors a transparent way to help specific families stay in their homes, with full visibility into where every dollar goes. The concept is built. The platform is next.

Concept

RethreadStudios

Artists revamp thrift clothing and give it away — building their portfolio while unsheltered neighbors get clean clothes.

RethreadStudios invites artists to transform secondhand clothing into wearable pieces, then give the finished pieces directly to unsheltered people — no strings attached. Every giveaway builds the artist's portfolio, turning generosity into real creative credit. In return, unsheltered neighbors receive clean, quality clothing made with care, not leftovers. Small in scale, real in impact.
