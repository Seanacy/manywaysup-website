In the Works

The platforms are built. The petition is live. Here's what's coming after the breakthrough.

Neighbors Grow

A future platform connecting community gardeners with people experiencing food insecurity. Neighbors with extra to share get matched with neighbors who need it — turning backyard gardens into a real food-access network.

RethreadStudios

Where Thrift Meets Art. A marketplace for artists who transform secondhand clothing into one-of-one pieces. Every garment has a story, every artist has a vision — and every piece ships with a QR tag linking back to it.

View the build on GitHub →

Halo Housing Project

A verified rent assistance platform. People facing eviction submit their case, it gets verified, then gets funded through pooled community donations, direct sponsorship from individual donors, and escrow-secured matching campaigns. Every dollar is tracked from donation to payout, with a public outcome report once a case is funded.
